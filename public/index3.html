WHO WE ARE

The Indian Sub-continental Club (ISC) at University of California, Irvine has been an influential and active non-profit student organization for over two decades. The goal of ISC is to unite the students of UC Irvine whose cultural roots come from that of the Indian Sub-continent: Bangladesh, Bhutan, India, the Maldives, Nepal, Pakistan, and Sri Lanka. Every year ISC holds numerous activities to spread cultural awareness and provide a place where students from similar backgrounds can meet and interact. It also provides an environment to stimulate cultural awareness through various ISC-sponsored activities and events, such as our Diwali party, Holi celebration, and Culture Show.

  • Culture Show
    Our biggest event of the spring quarter

    Culture show is one of the most popular events hosted by ISC, where hundreds of UCI students, regardless of ethnicity and dance experience, come together to put on a show celebrating Indian culture. Participating in culture show is a great way to learn about the traditions, heritage, and dances of India, as well as meet and bond with fellow ISC members. With dances ranging from Bharatanatyam, with classical southern Indian origins, to Fusion, which assimilates American and Indian cultures, there's something for everyone. An ISC membership is required to participate in culture show, and also comes with discounted entry to the after party, as well as various ISC events throughout the year!
